Double Integration Solver

Theory

Double integration extends integration to functions of two variables. It calculates area and volume over 2D regions.

∬ f(x,y)dA

Applications include engineering, physics, graphics and AI.

Worked Example

Question:

∬(x+2y)dA over R=[0,2]×[0,1]

Step 1:

∫₀¹ ∫₀² (x+2y) dxdy

Step 2:

[x²/2 + 2yx]₀²

Step 3:

2 + 4y

Final Answer: 4
